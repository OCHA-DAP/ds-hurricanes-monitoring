Columns reference:

  • Name: The name of the administrative division.
  • Max. wind in area (knots): The estimated maximum windspeed inside the administrative division, based on the forecast track. This is a simple estimate that does not take into account differences in wind speed between quadrants of the storm, which can be significant.
  • Similar historical storms (knots) The three historical storms since 2000 with the closest "Max. wind in area" to this forecast. The corresponding "Max. wind in area" of the historical storm is shown in brackets.
  • Estimated return period (years): Based on the "Max. wind in area" of the forecast, the approximate return period, based on historical storms since 2000.
  • Min. distance (km): The forecasted closest pass of the storm to the administrative division. 0 km would correspond to landfall.
  • Category while closest: The category of the storm while it is at its closest pass to the administrative division. Note that higher winds in the administrative may be generated by the storm while it is farther away but stronger.
  • Time to closest pass (hours): The estimated time from the issue time of the forecast (listed above) and the storm's closest pass to the administrative division.
